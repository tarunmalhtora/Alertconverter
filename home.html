About OpenSearch

OpenSearch is a community-driven project that allows you to search, analyze, and visualize your data in real time.

History

OpenSearch started as a fork of Elasticsearch, created after Elasticsearch changed its licensing model. It is fully open-source and supported by a diverse community.

Use Cases

  • Log and event data analysis
  • Real-time search applications
  • Business intelligence and analytics dashboards
